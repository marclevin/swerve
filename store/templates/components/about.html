About Us

Industry is again moving through the process of design and development. Renewables, IoT and Electric Vehicles, among others, are dramatically altering the way we cope and move with life. This change comes along with brilliant new products and services, important regulatory changes and the easing of everyday life.


This unfortunately comes with a few drawbacks. The very air we breathe is becoming steadily more toxic, the means we currently use to generate electricity is destroying countless ecosystems, and we are rapidly approaching the point of no return.


This is where Swerve comes in. Our solution to the toxic gasses released into the air, is the ease of access to electric vehicles. This, paired with renewable energy, couldbe the first step in finally beginning the reversal of climate change.


Browse our selection of electric vehicle alternatives, or take a look at our ingenious calculator in order to find the electric vehicle that will best fit your everyday travel requirements.
